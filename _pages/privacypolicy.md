# Privacy policy

**Last updated**  
7 June 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how LEVO handles your information. LEVO stores your game library on your device and does not collect it on LEVO servers.

## Information stored in LEVO

### Information you provide

- Games you add to your library, including titles, platforms, covers, status, and finish dates
- App preferences and settings

### Information LEVO does not collect

- Personal identification information such as your name, email, or phone number
- Location data
- Device contacts, photos, or other personal files
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics or tracking data

## How information is used

The information you enter is used only to:

- Display your game library
- Look up game metadata and cover art
- Generate game recommendations
- Save your app preferences

## Data storage and security

**Local storage:** Your library is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

**iCloud sync:** If iCloud is enabled for your Apple Account, Apple may sync your library across your devices using CloudKit. LEVO cannot access your private iCloud data.

**No LEVO server storage:** Your game library is not stored on LEVO servers.

## Third-party services

LEVO uses these services to provide core app features:

- **IGDB:** Used to look up game metadata and cover art in response to your searches. IGDB is a video game database provided by Twitch.
- **StoreKit:** Used to process the optional LEVO Unlimited purchase. Payment information is handled by Apple, not LEVO.
- **CloudKit:** Used by Apple to sync your local library between your devices when iCloud is enabled.

## Data sharing

LEVO does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers. Your library remains on your device and, if enabled, in your private iCloud account.

## Your rights

You have complete control over your data:

- All local app data can be deleted by removing the app
- Individual games can be removed within the app
- No account registration is required

## Children's privacy

LEVO is not intended for children under 13. LEVO does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
